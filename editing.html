Process

A clear, efficient workflow from assembly through delivery.

  • I assemble the edit in Avid using organized selects and pulls.
  • I refine rhythm and structure through editorial passes with annotated review exports.
  • I build temp sound and graphics, with temp color created in DaVinci Resolve.
  • I deliver final masters in ProRes and H.264.
